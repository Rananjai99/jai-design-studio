FONT FILES REQUIRED — place these in this folder (/public/fonts/)
-----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Langdon.woff2
2. Langdon.woff
   → Used for: all headers, page titles, tagline ("LATITUDE OF HOME")
   → Obtain from: https://www.dafont.com/langdon.font or your licensed copy

3. Moon.woff2
4. Moon.woff
   → Used for: EN | HINDI language toggle, colour picker list
   → Obtain from: https://www.dafont.com/moon-2-0.font or your licensed copy

Montserrat is loaded via Google Fonts in globals.css — no file needed.

Without these files the site will fall back to system sans-serif fonts.
The layout will still work, only the custom typography will differ.
